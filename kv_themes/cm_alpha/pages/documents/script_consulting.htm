As artists and creative minds, we get very excited about our work. We forget, however, that we can’t always spot our own mistakes and weaknesses. As a result, while our ideas may be crystal clear in our minds, they may not come shining through on the page. That’s where I come in.

 

Through my education and my independent film making experiences, I’ve been assisting writers and filmmakers in strengthening their work for over ten years. I understand the ins and outs of helping writers work through their projects in order to gain a greater understanding of their characters and plots as well as coaching them on writing compelling stories, helping them to strengthen and to professionally polish their own screenplays. 

 

Feel free to email me at any time with any questions!

 

With script consulting I will:

·      Require you to submit a synopsis (3 paragraphs for a feature, 3-6 paragraphs for a pilot, 1 paragraph for a short film) prior to reading your script.

·      Focus on the quality, not the quantity, of your writing.

·      Help you examine your characters’ motivations and to create in depth, compelling characters.

·      Go through your work act by act and ensure you are hitting the necessary beats.

·      Point out any weaknesses in your narrative.

·      Analyze the pacing of your story

·      Ask a lot of questions that don’t necessarily require an immediate answer but are meant for you to analyze your own writing decisions and make tough choices about your screenplay.

·      Guide you to professionally polishing your own work.

·      Give you some reading suggestions that may help you on your individual project and improve overall as a writer.

·      Provide brainstorming activities that can help you better understand your own work.

·      Focus on the franchise (for Pilots).

 

I will not:

·      Tell you what to write or write anything for you.

·      Necessarily adhere to the “industry standard”.

·      Teach you about act structures or script formats.

·      Provide you with coverage.

·      Provide you with written comments. You should be taking notes!        

·      Help you make connections in the industry.

·      Tell you what to do with your script after it is polished.

Payments

Non Disclosure Agreements

I’m happy to sign an NDA for you, but you must provide it prior to sending me your materials. I do not provide them for you.

 

Rescheduling

Life happens! If you need to reschedule your consultation, please do so twenty-four hours in advance.

 

Refund Policy

If you have made a payment but decide not to do the phone consultation session, you must request a refund forty-eight hours before our scheduled consultation time. No refunds will be given after that.

 

 

Social Responsibility

**I do not consult on scripts with stories that center on the victimization of women or that represent negative portrayals the majority of female characters in story. (e.g. if over half your female characters are prostitutes, ethnic stereotypes, domestic abuse victims, overly sexed individuals that become victims, victims of serial perpetrators, etc.)**

 

Being a volunteer Violence Prevention Specialist who advocates media done responsibly, I will not work on your project if the story centers on the victimization of women for entertainment. I will also refuse your project if the majority of women in your film are portrayed negatively (e.g. as whores, prostitutes, dumb blonds, ethnic stereotypes, domestic abuse victims, victims of serial perpetrators, overly sexed women who end up victims, etc.). 

 

While I am generally not opposed to violence in films, I like to know that the work I do does not advocate violence against women. If you are not sure whether or not your film fits into this category, just ask! I’ll be happy to tell you what I think without judgment of your creative idea. It’s nothing personal, but not every project is for me.
